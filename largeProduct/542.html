Hyperlash Mascara

23.0

When it comes to lashes, our L.A. photo studio needs low-maintenance and high-drama. We created this all-in-1 mascara with a unique applicator that has a brush on one side and a comb on the other to bring you long, full, clump-free lashes in seconds. Rich pigments bind to lashes for an ultra-intense look. Be totally photo-fabulous—on camera and off!De-curl unruly lashes by dragging mascara from top side down. Dual brush & comb applicator Intense deep-black pigments Use the brush to build and thicken. Follow with the comb side to define and lengthen.
