Anna Sui Nail Colour

15.0

This Anna Sui Nail Colour is available in an array of fashion-forward colours and textures.  This dazzling nail colour is long lasting and luxurious and has a delicate Tea Rose scent. Directions: Apply a clear base coat. Start right in the centre of the nail, then apply on the two sides of the nails.  Make sure that the entire nail is covered in these 3 swipes.  Let the base coat dry completely.  Once base coat has dried apply the colour of your choice in a similar fashion: first on the centre of the nail and then on the sides.  Once dry, apply a top coat.
